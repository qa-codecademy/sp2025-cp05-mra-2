Our Mission

At our core, our mission is to provide exceptional facility management services that ensure safe, efficient, and sustainable environments for everyone who uses our spaces. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness, functionality, and comfort in every building we manage. By leveraging modern technologies and a proactive approach, we aim to reduce costs, extend asset life, and improve the overall experience of tenants and visitors. Our dedicated team works with integrity, responsibility, and a strong focus on client satisfaction, delivering tailored solutions that support operational goals and contribute to a better, more productive workplace.

Our Location

Our facility management company is proudly based in the heart of Switzerland, strategically located to serve businesses and properties across the region with speed and efficiency. You can find us easily using the map provided on our website, or by searching our company name on Google Maps. Whether you're located in Zurich, Bern, Geneva, or nearby areas, our team is just a short drive away and ready to support your facility needs. We welcome visitors by appointment and are always happy to provide directions or assistance. Your trusted facility partner is always within reach in Switzerland.

Our Services

At MrAllAround, we offer a full suite of facility management services tailored to keep your spaces clean, functional, and secure. Our Cleaning Services ensure spotless environments through daily cleaning, window washing, and floor care using eco-friendly products. Our Maintenance Services cover HVAC support, electrical repairs, and plumbing checks to keep everything running smoothly. We also provide reliable Security Services, including 24/7 surveillance, access control, and emergency response to protect your property and people. With a dedicated team and attention to detail, we deliver solutions that enhance safety, comfort, and operational efficiency.
